Plan & Path — strategy + weekly execution

Your long-term AI-resilient path up top; the weekly pipeline & checklist to execute it below.
🧭 AI-resilient growth path
Honest verdict: the two-track plan points the right way but is not yet "perfect." A dbt/Snowflake pivot on its own lands in the layer AI compresses fastest — data/analytics postings fell ~15% YoY through late 2025 and code-gen builds routine pipelines 45–50% faster. The fix isn't a new destination; it's adding the two things AI can't replicate and you half-own: industrial-manufacturing domain depth + architecture ownership, then aiming the pivot at data-for-AI & governance, not commodity ETL.
The one principle
AI automates the typing — not the judgment, the domain, or the accountability. The 2026 premium goes to AI fluency stacked on deep domain expertise (~56% more than peers). Your scarce asset isn't "PL/SQL" — it's that you can read a steel plant. Most data engineers can't. That's the moat.
Where AI WILL cut the road — avoid as a destination
Pure PL/SQL CRUD, hand-coded stored procs, report writing — commodity code-gen territory.
Generic ETL pipeline assembly with no domain or governance layer on top.
Manual DBA / routine maintenance — self-healing, automated databases are here.
Where the road stays open — aim here
Industrial / manufacturing data architecture — genuinely scarce; your metals / APS-MES / Material-Allocator background is the differentiator.
Data platform & governance — quality, lineage, contracts. The upstream problem AI adoption creates more of.
Data-for-AI — clean, governed data + retrieval layers that feed RAG systems and agents.
Orchestrating AI, not competing with it — owning architecture, tradeoffs and accountability.
The path — "Domain-Anchored Data/AI Engineer"

Phase 0 · now–3 mo

Stabilise cash, harvest the moat

Keep applying Track A (Oracle EBS / OIC-Fusion, ₹14–22L) — it funds the pivot and OIC/Fusion is itself an up-move. Reframe every PL/SQL / PSI Metals win as "I move, model & govern high-volume industrial data."

Phase 1 · 3–9 mo

Bridge skills

Python + advanced SQL → dbt → Snowflake plus the AI-durable add-ons: data modelling, Airflow, and data quality / governance. Portfolio: a manufacturing analytics pipeline (MES data → Snowflake → dbt → dashboard).

Phase 2 · 9–18 mo

Add the AI layer

Vector DBs + embeddings; one RAG pipeline over industrial / maintenance docs; basic LLM + agent orchestration (LangChain). Target title: Data Engineer (AI / Platform). Reaches the GenAI-adjacent band (₹20–70L) without becoming a research scientist.

Phase 3 · 18 mo+

Compound into architecture

Data Platform Engineer / Data Architect / "AI-data" lead — the judgment-and-accountability seat AI doesn't take. Remote-international (₹28–42L+) becomes realistic.
